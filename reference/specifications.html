History


The iPad Pro 11-inch (3rd generation) and iPad Pro 12.9-inch (5th generation) were announced on April 20, 2021, with the same design and screen sizes as the previous generation. They offer the Apple M1 chip, 5G connectivity, Thunderbolt 3/USB 4 connectivity (up to 40 Gbit/s), external display output up to 6K resolution, 8 or 16 GB of RAM (16 GB is only available for 1 TB and 2 TB models), and, on the 12.9" variant, an XDR display with mini-LEDs marketed as "Liquid Retina XDR". Preorders began on April 30, 2021, and the product was released on May 21, 2021.
